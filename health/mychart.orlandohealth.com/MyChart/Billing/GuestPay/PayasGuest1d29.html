What is a guarantor? The guarantor is the person responsible for paying the bill. You can find the guarantor name and account number on your statement.
What is a visit account? A visit account is how your charges are grouped together to bill insurance, and usually represents your billing activity for a single visit. You may have multiple visit accounts, each with their own balance.
